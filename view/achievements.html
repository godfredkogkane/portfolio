Achievements/Awards

The Coca-Cola Foundation Social Impact Fund, Ashesi University

The Coca-Cola Foundation recently initiated a partnership to bolster students' community center initiatives through the provision of grants. Among the esteemed Coca-Cola Foundation Social Impact Fund recipients is the IT Access project at Ashesi University. This significant achievement signifies a beacon of hope for numerous underprivileged students, empowering them with practical education and enabling them to surmount poverty in an era where technology catalyzes progress.

Duration: Jan 2023 - Jan 2024

The MasterCard Foundation Scholar Program Scholarship, Ashesi University

The Ashesi MasterCard, Foundation Scholars Program, affords exceptional and disadvantaged students access to a first-rate education. Its primary goal is to equip these students with the necessary skills and knowledge to catalyze progress and advancement within their communities throughout Africa. I am privileged to have received a comprehensive scholarship to pursue a degree in Management Information Systems.

Duration: Sep 2021 - Jul 2025

Ford Foundation Fund for Service to Children and Youth, Ashesi University

This prestigious opportunity, generously funded by the Ford Foundation, provides students with financial support, comprehensive training, and personalized coaching to successfully execute projects to improve the lives of underprivileged children and youth.

Duration: Nov 2021 - Dec 2023
