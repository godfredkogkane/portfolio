Bio

Godfred Kogkane (Ghana) is a Management Information Systems student at Ashesi University, Ghana, set to graduate in December 2025. He has demonstrated exceptional dedication to community service as a scholar under The MasterCard Foundation Scholar Program. He is currently a Marketing Intern at the African Leadership Academy in South Africa. Here, he produces engaging digital content for the AL for the Arts initiative, strategically aligning media plans with organizational goals. Additionally, Godfred has contributed significantly to the field of communications as a Communications Intern at the African Film & Arts Foundation Inc. in Atlanta, GA. He managed and updated foundation websites, developed newsletters, and maintained a professional online presence on LinkedIn. His commitment to social impact extends to Witkoppen Clinic in South Africa, where he currently serves as a Business Development Intern, contributing to compelling grant proposals and refining fundraising strategies. Beyond his academic and professional pursuits, Godfred is a Co-founder of the IT Access Project in Accra, Ghana, overseeing technical aspects and coordination to provide practical technological knowledge to over 500 students.
